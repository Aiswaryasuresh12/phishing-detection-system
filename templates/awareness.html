🔐 Phishing Awareness

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyber attack where attackers trick users into revealing sensitive information such as passwords or bank details.

Common Signs of Phishing

  • Suspicious website URLs
  • Fake login pages
  • Urgent messages asking for personal information
  • Spelling mistakes in websites
  • Unknown email links

How to Stay Safe

  • Always check HTTPS in the URL
  • Verify the website domain
  • Do not click suspicious links
  • Enable two-factor authentication
  • Never share passwords or OTP
